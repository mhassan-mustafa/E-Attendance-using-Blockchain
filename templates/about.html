About

Low ratio of attendance of college students in their classes is one of the most challenging problems being faced by college management. Moreover, fake issuance of degrees without attending college is also on the rise around the world. Among various solutions, implementation of student attendance system through blockchain technology is considered the most suitable, in this regard.


E-attendance system for students with the blockchain methodology can keep the data immutable that cannot be altered or changed by any means. The system will provide permanent record of lecture attendance that can be helpful for evaluation purposes. The attendance once marked cannot be updated and data is stored safely at all connected locations. The record is transparent and accessible through all connected nodes. The system cannot be controlled centrally or no intermediary party will be required. It is automation with secured unidirectional encryption.


This system will provide separate interfaces to students, faculty and management for their assigned transactions. It is based on mining of blocks with unique IDs and proof of work before adding the blocks to main database. Hashing technique will be used to certify digital signatures of students. Students will log in to their portal and mark the attendance against the classes where they are authorized. Moreover, management would define the attendance option against particular classes where the students are enrolled. A user-friendly simple web-based interface will be developed both for the students and management.
